---
description: Review learned drafts and promote the useful ones into real skills
---

Review the learned drafts produced by continuous learning and promote the durable ones into one or more real skills.

Additional context: $ARGUMENTS

Required behavior:

1. Resolve the active learned directory from `skills/continuous-learning/config.json`.
2. Browse the available `*.draft.md` files.
3. Cluster duplicates or near-duplicates.
4. Promote only the high-signal drafts into real skills under the configured skills root.
5. Mark promoted drafts with `status: promoted` and `promoted_to: [..]`.
6. Leave weak drafts untouched and report why they were skipped.
